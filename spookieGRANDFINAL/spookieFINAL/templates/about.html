A quick glimpse of our project

Object detection is a crucial aspect of computer vision, facilitating instant recognition and sorting of objects in images and videos. This project employs YOLO (You Only Look Once) for object detection utilizing Ultralytics YOLOv8 and Roboflow for preparing the dataset and training the model. The model is trained on a specified dataset and deployed for real-time object detection through a live camera feed. When an object is detected, it is included in a list, and its price is retrieved from the dataset. Notable obstacles such as inaccurate detections, variability in lighting conditions, and constraints in the dataset have been identified. Potential enhancements could involve improving model precision, diversifying the dataset, and incorporating additional functionalities to establish a more resilient and scalable billing framework. This endeavor underscores the promise of AI-powered automation in retail and object-centric pricing estimations.
